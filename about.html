ABOUT ROTARY

Rotary unites more than a million people

Rotary is a global network of 1.2 million neighbors, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.

Solving real problems takes real commitment and vision. For more than 110 years, Rotary's people of action have used their passion, energy, and intelligence to take action on sustainable projects. From literacy and peace to water and health, we are always working to better our world, and we stay committed to the end.

Rotarians believe that “one profits most who serves best,” and they are committed to creating inroads with people so that opportunities to serve will arise from those connections. The Rotarian philosophy is that mutual service is the best way to create thriving businesses and societies. A Rotary club is also committed to ethical practices in business and holds high ideals for personal behavior.

ABOUT ROTARACT

Global Movement Of Young Leaders

Rotaract Club of Pune was chartered by parent Rotary Club of Aundh in the year 2014. Our team started with a handful of members. Currently, we have entered in the 7th year and we have expanded on all social handles at zonal, district, and international levels.

In all these years, our club members have used their passion, dedication, energy, intelligence, and selfless devotion on multiple sustainable projects.

From cleanliness drives and water harvesting projects to hosting events such as the sanitary pad awareness drives in our community to ice breaker events and events that increase bonding among the entire district such as the Phoenix Cricket League.

Our club also aims and considers Professional Development as a top avenue along with the community service. We’ve had various sessions on various different subjects through the year that would help us grow, give us knowledge, and make us ready to be a better person in our personal and professional lives. We believe in working togther for the betterment of the community and, in return, making ourselves better as well.
